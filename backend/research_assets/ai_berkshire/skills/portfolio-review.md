# Portfolio Review — Systematic Portfolio Assessment

## Skill Description
Conduct a structured review of the entire investment portfolio, examining concentration, diversification, thesis validity, and action priorities.

## Review Philosophy
A good portfolio review is not a celebration — it is a disciplined audit. The goal is to find what is wrong, what has changed, and what requires action before the market forces your hand.

## Review Framework

### Section 1 — Portfolio Structure Analysis
- **Concentration check**: Largest single position as % of total. Flag if > 30%.
- **Sector / industry distribution**: Are more than 60% of assets in correlated industries?
- **Geographic / currency distribution**: FX exposure summary.
- **Liquidity assessment**: How quickly could 50% of the portfolio be liquidated at fair value?
- **Cash allocation**: Current cash %. Is it deliberate or inertia?

### Section 2 — Per-Holding Thesis Review
For each holding, answer in order:
1. **Original thesis**: What was the core reason to own this?
2. **Thesis status**: Still valid / Partially valid / Invalidated — and why?
3. **New information**: What facts have emerged since purchase that were not anticipated?
4. **Price vs. value**: Has the stock price moved ahead of intrinsic value, or lagged?
5. **Action signal**: Add / Hold / Reduce / Exit — with specific trigger conditions

### Section 3 — Cross-Portfolio Risks
- Which 2–3 holdings are most correlated? Is this concentration intentional?
- What is the single macro scenario (rate spike, credit crunch, regulatory shock) that would most damage this portfolio?
- Which holding has the greatest risk of permanent capital loss (not just price volatility)?

### Section 5 — Portfolio Health Score
Rate the overall portfolio on 4 dimensions (1–10):
- **Quality**: Are the underlying businesses genuinely high-quality?
- **Valuation**: Is the portfolio priced at a reasonable discount to intrinsic value?
- **Diversification**: Is risk appropriately spread?
- **Clarity**: Do you have high conviction in the thesis for each position?

## Output Format
Deliver a structured report with all 5 sections. Conclude with a 3-bullet executive summary of the most important findings and the single most important action to take in the next 30 days.
